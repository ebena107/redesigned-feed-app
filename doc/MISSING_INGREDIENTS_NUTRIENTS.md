### Data Quality

- **High Priority (1-5):** Complete industrial-standard data from NRC and Feedipedia
- **Medium Priority (6-9):** Good quality data from peer-reviewed sources
- **Low Priority (10-20):** Limited data, may need manual supplementation

### Recommended Action

1. **Add ingredients 1-9** to new dataset with full nutritional profiles
2. **Skip ingredients 10-20** unless user specifically requests them
3. **Total new dataset:** 120 (current) + 9 (added) = **129 ingredients**

### Format Compatibility

All data is formatted to match the new industrial dataset structure:
- Nested amino acid objects (total + SID)
- Nested energy object with 7 animal types
- Phosphorus breakdown (total, available, phytate)
- Anti-nutritional factors where applicable
- Bulk density estimates based on similar ingredients
