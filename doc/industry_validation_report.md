## Validation Methodology

### Tolerance Levels

- **Acceptable Range:** ±10% from published standards
- **Minor Warning:** 5-15% deviation
- **Major Issue:** >15% deviation

### Rationale for Tolerance

Natural variation in feed ingredients is expected due to:
- Growing conditions (soil, climate, variety)
- Processing methods
- Storage conditions
- Analytical method differences
- Regional variations

Industry practice accepts 10-15% variation as normal for biological materials.
